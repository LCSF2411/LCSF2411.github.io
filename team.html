Our Story

Founded in 1998, ArcBlade Works has grown from a small workshop in Sarnia, Ontario, to a trusted name in the power tool industry. Our vision of delivering high-performance circular saws and innovative power tools has been at the core of our success. With a commitment to quality and sustainability, we continue to lead the way in creating cutting-edge tools that meet the evolving needs of professionals and DIY enthusiasts alike.

Our Vision for the Future

Looking ahead, we plan to expand our product range, introduce new technologies, and continue our commitment to customer satisfaction. We aim to redefine the standards of quality in the power tool industry, providing users with innovative and reliable tools for years to come.
